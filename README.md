## 🧸 Project 1 — ABC Toys: Sales & Inventory Analysis

**Context:** ABC Toys is a retail toy chain with 50 stores across 29 cities.
As part of the Data Analyst team, I built a full EDA report for the year-end
strategy meeting, covering 21 months of sales data (Jan 2022 – Sep 2023).

**Key questions answered:**
1. Revenue & profit trends — any seasonality?
2. YoY comparison: 2023 vs 2022 (first 9 months)
3. Which product category has the best margin?
4. Product quadrant matrix: Stars, Cash Cows, Dogs
5. Which store location type performs best per store?
6. Top cities by revenue — how concentrated?
7. Inventory health — which products are out of stock?

**Tools:** Python · pandas · matplotlib · seaborn

**Files:**
- `ABC_Toys_EDA.ipynb` — full analysis notebook
- `ABC_Toys_Presentation.pptx` — executive summary slides
- `sales.csv`, `stores.csv`, `products.csv`, `inventory.xlsx` — source data
